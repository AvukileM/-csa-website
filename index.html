The 90-minute Cornerstone Session

15 minutes of mentorship. 60 minutes of focused tutoring.

Each lesson runs for one full hour on Zoom. The first 15 minutes are intentionally dedicated to mentorship and academic coaching, where the tutor checks in with the learner, discusses goals, confidence, study habits and challenges, then helps the learner prepare mentally for the academic work ahead.

The remaining 60 minutes are used for personalised one-on-one or group tutoring aligned with the learner’s curriculum, pace and academic needs.

Online learning platform

Live, interactive lessons on Zoom.

All CSA sessions are delivered live on Zoom, making high-quality tutoring accessible from anywhere in South Africa. Learners can ask questions, work through examples, use screen sharing and engage directly with their tutor in real time.

All that is needed is a stable internet connection and a phone, tablet or computer.

Questions parents ask

Clear answers before you begin.

Do you tutor online?

Yes. All lessons are conducted live on Zoom, making support accessible wherever the learner is based.

Which grades do you teach?

We support high school learners, with a focus on Grades 8–12.

Which subjects do you offer?

We offer Mathematics, Physical Sciences, Life Sciences, Geography, CAT, Accounting, English, Business Studies and more depending on tutor availability.

How long is each session?

Each session runs for 90 minutes: approximately 15 minutes of mentorship and academic coaching, followed by 60 minutes of focused tutoring.

How do I book a session?

Simply click the “Book a Consultation” button on this page to schedule a free consultation. We will discuss your child’s needs and recommend the best programme.

How do I pay for sessions?

We offer simple monthly plans. Payment is made via EFT, and we will provide all the details during your consultation.

What if my child needs a different subject?

We can often accommodate additional subjects depending on tutor availability. Please discuss your child’s needs during the consultation.

Who are the tutors?

Our tutors are high-achieving university students selected for both academic ability and mentorship potential.
